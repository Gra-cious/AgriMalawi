## Project Name
AgriMalawi

## Project Code
AGM-001

## Vision

To empower farmers, aspiring farmers, and agricultural entrepreneurs in Malawi by providing a complete digital platform that guides them through every stage of a Crop Project—from planning and budgeting to cultivation and harvest.

## Mission

To make agricultural knowledge accessible, practical, and easy to follow through a modern mobile-first web application.

## Core Values

- Simplicity
- Accuracy
- Accessibility
- Practicality
- Innovation

## Primary Audience

Beginners with little or no farming experience who want to successfully start and manage agricultural projects.

## Long-Term Goal

To become Malawi's leading digital agriculture platform and expand to support additional countries in Africa.
